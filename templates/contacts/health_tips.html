Daily Health Tips

Small habits: big impact on your well-being

Drink 6-8 cups of water every day.

Sleep 7-9 hours to support immunity & brain health.

Get regular check-ups, prevention saves lives.

Laugh, socialize & manage stress intentionally.

Take phone-free breaks throughout the day

Reduce your salt intake to 5g per day, equivalent to about one teaspoon.

Smoking tobacco causes NCDs such as lung disease, heart disease and stroke. Tobacco kills not only the direct smokers but even non-smokers through second-hand exposure.

A 15-minute walk improves mood and focus

Have your blood pressure checked regularly by a health worker so you know your numbers.

Make sure you only take antibiotics if prescribed by a qualified health professional.
